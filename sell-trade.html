Get the best offer

A few small things move the needle.

  • Bring everything. Original box, manual, inserts, all cables and controllers — complete items always offer more.
  • Test before you bring. If you know it works, say so. We’ll still test, but it speeds things up.
  • Clean is better. A wipe-down on consoles and a quick look through card binders helps us move faster.
  • Sleeve your singles. Cards in penny sleeves or a binder get valued more efficiently than loose stacks.
  • Don’t toss the “junk.” Bring the broken stuff, the loose discs, the obscure accessories. You’d be surprised what we’ll buy.
