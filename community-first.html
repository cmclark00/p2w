How it works

No markup. No scalping. Just our community first.

When a new core set releases, our consistent in-store players can grab core set product at true MSRP. It’s how we say thank you to the people who show up for our TCG events, locals, and trade nights.

  • One core set item per person At MSRP, while release allocation lasts.
  • For our consistent in-store players The regulars at our events, locals, and trade nights.
  • Must be present to purchase No holds, proxies, or buying on someone else’s behalf.
  • We cut the seal at pickup Product goes home ready to open and play.

Not sure if you qualify? Just ask our staff — if you’re a familiar face at the tables, you probably do.
