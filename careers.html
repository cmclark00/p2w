Want to work with us?

We hire people who actually play the games and care about the customer across the counter. If that sounds like you, fill out the form below and email your resume — we’ll be in touch.

What we’re looking for

People who play.

Counter & Sales

Greet customers, ring up sales, talk shop, and keep the floor running. Comfort with retail POS and product knowledge across video games / TCG / consoles is a plus.

TCG Specialist

Pricing singles, sorting trade-ins, running events, knowing your game inside-out. Pokémon, Magic, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Lorcana, One Piece — pick your lane.

Repair Tech

Console and handheld repair bench. Soldering experience preferred but not required for entry-level — we’ll train. Patience and a steady hand matter more than a certification.

Event Coordinator

Running tournaments, league nights, release parties. Familiarity with TOM, Cardio, or Bandai TCG+ helps. Weekends are the busy nights here.
