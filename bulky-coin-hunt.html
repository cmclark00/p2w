Saturdays at Play2Win

Bulky’s Coin Hunt

Every Saturday, Bulky loses one of his coins somewhere within 5 miles of the shop. Find it, bring it to us, and it’s worth $50 in store credit.

How it works

One coin, somewhere out there, every Saturday.

It looks exactly like the coin up top — the Play2Win Bulky coin. Keep your eyes open around town and you might be the one to bring it home.

  • Hidden within 5 miles Every Saturday, Bulky drops a coin somewhere inside a 5-mile radius of 3903 Western Avenue.
  • Find the coin It’s the same Bulky coin pictured on this page. Spot it, grab it — it’s yours.
  • $50 store credit Bring the coin to the counter and we’ll load $50 of store credit onto your account.

Rain check. If the weather’s bad, Bulky decides to stay indoors — so on nasty-weather Saturdays he doesn’t lose a coin and there’s nothing to find. Not sure? Check our Facebook or Discord before you head out.
