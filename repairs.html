Repairs & Upgrades

Console and handheld repair in Knoxville.

Bring in your broken, dead, or damaged gear. We handle screen replacements, battery service, disc resurfacing, controller fixes, and board-level repairs — plus handheld mods.

What we fix

Repair services we offer.

Screen Replacement

Cracked, dead, or dim screens on handhelds and portables swapped out and tested before return.

Battery Service

Dead or swollen batteries replaced on handhelds, controllers, and portable consoles.

Disc Resurfacing

Scratched discs cleaned and polished to get your games loading again.

Console & Controller Repair

Stick drift, stuck buttons, dirty contacts, disc drive issues, and general diagnostics.

How a repair works

Three steps, no surprises.

01

Drop It Off

Bring it in and pay a $30 non-refundable diagnosis fee. That gets it onto the bench and in queue.

02

We Diagnose

Our techs figure out exactly what’s wrong and source parts if needed. Usually within 2 weeks, often faster.

03

You Decide

We call with the full quote — your $30 already counts toward it. Green-light and we finish the job; pass and you walk away knowing what was wrong.
